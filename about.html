About Mbogiwood Productions

Mbogiwood Productions is dedicated to telling authentic Kenyan stories through film, giving a voice to narratives often overlooked. Our mission is to revolutionize the Kenyan film industry by creating high-quality, culturally diverse productions accessible to all.

Our Vision

To establish Mbogiwood as the 'Hollywood of Kenya and Africa', fostering a thriving creative industry for African storytellers.

Our Mission

Empowering Kenyan creatives by providing affordable film production resources, ensuring timely content distribution, and producing culturally relevant films.
